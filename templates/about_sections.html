About Sunny Side Burrow School

Our story

Sunny Side Burrow School is a welcoming learning community where students grow with confidence, curiosity, and care for one another. We combine strong academics with character formation so every learner is prepared for the next step—whether that is higher education or a chosen path in life.

Mission

To provide a safe, supportive environment where students can discover their strengths, pursue excellence, and develop the habits of lifelong learning.

Vision

A school community known for integrity, innovation, and inclusive excellence—where every student is known, challenged, and celebrated.

What we value

  • Respect — for self, others, and our shared spaces
  • Responsibility — for learning, attendance, and honest work
  • Collaboration — teachers, families, and students working together
  • Resilience — learning from mistakes and trying again
